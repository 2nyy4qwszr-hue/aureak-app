## Bug-hunter (cas limites)

### Route inconnue (ex: `/foo/bar`)
- `parseBreadcrumbs('/foo/bar', {})` → `[{label:'foo', href:'/foo', isActive:false}, {label:'bar', href:'/foo/bar', isActive:true}]`
- Segment inconnu → label brut du segment. Comportement sûr, pas de crash.

### 1 seul segment (ex: `/children`)
- `parseBreadcrumbs('/children', {})` → `[{label:'Joueurs', href:'/children', isActive:true}]`
- `items.length <= 1` → `return null` → breadcrumb non rendu. Correct.

### Clic sur le dernier segment
- Dernier segment (`isActive: true`) rendu comme `<Text>` non-pressable. Aucun handler onPress. Correct.
